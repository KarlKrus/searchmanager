Push to Production

The Push to Production module includes all the rules that are already published and rules yet to publish. All created rules will not take effect unless approved and pushed to production. On the other hand, all published rules can still be pulled down from production.

Publishing Guidelines

To push previously approved rules to production:

  • In the Show dropdown box, select Approved Rules for Publishing.
  • When the list has refreshed, tick all applicable rules.
  • Provide notes in the Comment box and click the Publish button.
